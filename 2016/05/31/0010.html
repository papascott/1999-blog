Monthly archives
by Scott Hanson Tuesday, May 31, 2016

As announced at blog.1999.io, 1999.io now produces monthly archive pages. If your sever is up to date, the archive will be automatically saved under /users/username/year/month/index.html when you publish (or re-publish). My May archive is http://1999.papascott.de/2016/05/.

If you're styling, there's a new divArchivePageTitle to add to your style sheets.
